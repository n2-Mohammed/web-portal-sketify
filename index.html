How can i Get Verified Badge On Projects?
If the project is following terms and conditions of Sketify, it will eligible for verified badge. Our Team has to manually review you project, so the verification process might be delayed. Note: You will Loss Your verified badge when you update something that is out of terms & conditions.
Am I allowed to upload projects made through Sketchware Mods?
Currently, we don't allow projects made through mods. You can, however, upload APKs made with mods.
