Sketify is a unofficial app created for sharing Sketchware projects and apps. By uploading your projects and apps on Sketify, you can get comments from users about your app or project (or) how can you improve it. You can also get a good user base in your app, and there are many more advantages. Sketify has 2-3 daily active users and a user base of over 1k users.
